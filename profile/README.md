## About

Nivara is a **research-driven Generative AI company** headquartered in Amsterdam. We design, build, and operate end-to-end AI systems — from data ingestion and model development to cloud infrastructure and production deployment.

We don't ship prototypes. Every solution we deliver runs in production, processes real enterprise data, and is built for compliance, explainability, and scale from day one.

## Solutions

### Enterprise AI Products

| Solution | Industry | Description |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Nivara Navigator** | Needs Assessment | Interactive DAG-based planning engine, multi-tier document extraction (OCR + Vision AI), post-assessment analytics with AI chat, OSINT research, and export-ready reports |
| **Nivara OTB** | Luxury Retail | Open-to-Buy intelligence — 10 modules (Forecasting, Allocation, In-Season, Supply Chain, Costs, Approvals), 30 canvas views, AI-driven recommendations with full explainability |
| **Nivara Lobby** | Legal & Government | Legislative amendment analysis — extract, classify with confidence scoring, and diff decreto-legge amendments using multi-provider LLMs with Explainable AI |
| **Nivara Health** | Healthcare & IoT | Elder care monitoring — IoT sensor fusion, AI-driven behavioral analysis, predictive health analytics, fall detection (YOLO v8), and smart home automation |
| **Nivara Tender** | Public Procurement | Tender management — search Italian public tenders, match products with AI, extract specification clauses, acquire documents automatically, track awarded contracts |
| **Nivara Logistic** | Logistics | Supply chain intelligence — AI-powered document processing, automated multi-format extraction, and shipment tracking |
| **Nivara Rental** | Automotive | AI-powered lead generation and marketing intelligence for rental car fleets |

### Platform & Infrastructure

| Platform | What It Does |

## Key Principles

**Data Sovereignty** — Your data stays in your infrastructure. Always.

**Explainability** — Every AI output ships with confidence scores and reasoning chains. No black boxes.

**Production-First** — Scale, reliability, and compliance are not afterthoughts. They are the starting point.

**Multi-Provider** — No vendor lock-in. We orchestrate across OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Google, Groq, and open-source models.
