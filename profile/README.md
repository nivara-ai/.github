## How We Engage

```
DISCOVER → DESIGN → BUILD → OPERATE
```

We start with a structured needs assessment to map your data landscape and AI opportunities. We then architect and build the right system — not the most complex one. Every delivery includes operational runbooks, monitoring, and a path to self-sufficiency.

---

## Our Principles

**Data Sovereignty** — Your data stays in your infrastructure. Always. We architect for GDPR compliance and European data residency from the first commit.

**Explainability** — Every AI output ships with confidence scores and reasoning chains. No black boxes in production.

**Production-First** — Scale, reliability, and compliance are not afterthoughts. They are the starting point. We build for the constraints that matter.

**Multi-Provider AI** — No vendor lock-in. We orchestrate across OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, Google, Groq, and open-source models — choosing the right model for each task.
