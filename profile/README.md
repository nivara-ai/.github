## Who We Are

We are a **research-driven Generative AI company** headquartered in Amsterdam, building production-grade AI systems for enterprises across Europe.

We don't do demos. We ship **AI that runs in production**, processes real data, and delivers measurable business outcomes — with full compliance, explainability, and data sovereignty.

## What We Build

We design and deliver **end-to-end AI solutions** — from data ingestion and model development to cloud infrastructure and deployment.

### Enterprise AI Solutions

| Solution | Domain | What It Does |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Nivara Navigator** | Needs Assessment | AI-powered assessment platform with interactive DAG-based planning, multi-tier document extraction (OCR + Vision AI), post-assessment analytics with AI chat, and OSINT research |
| **Nivara OTB** | Luxury Retail | Open-to-Buy intelligence platform — 10 modules including forecasting, allocation, supply chain, cost analysis, and AI-driven recommendations with full explainability |
| **Nivara Lobby** | Legal & Government | Legislative amendment analysis with Explainable AI — extract, classify with confidence scoring, and diff decreto-legge amendments using multi-provider LLMs |
| **Nivara Health** | Healthcare & IoT | Integrated health monitoring and home automation for elder care — IoT devices, AI-driven behavioral analysis, predictive analytics, and smart automation |
| **Nivara Logistic** | Finance | AI-powered invoice extraction — automated document processing with multi-format support |

### Internal Platform & Infrastructure

| Platform | Purpose |
|:---|:---|
| **Nivara Portal** | Internal Developer Platform (Backstage) — software catalog, TechDocs, scaffolder, Tech Radar, RBAC, quality scorecards, and hybrid RAG AI assistant |
| **Nivara Playground** | Unified Enterprise AI POC portal — dynamic dashboard, AI Project Chat, multi-provider AI Playground, authentication with RBAC |
| **IaC & GitOps** | Multi-cloud infrastructure (AWS + GCP) managed with Terraform, deployed via ArgoCD |

## Key Principles

- **Data Sovereignty** — Your data never leaves your infrastructure. We build AI that runs where your data lives.
- **Explainability** — Every AI decision comes with confidence scores and reasoning. No black boxes.
- **Production-First** — We don't build prototypes. Every solution is designed for scale, reliability, and compliance from day one.
- **Multi-Provider** — No vendor lock-in. Our systems work across OpenAI, DeepSeek, open-source models, and custom fine-tuned models.
